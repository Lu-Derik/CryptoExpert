# Crypto Daily Insight - 2026-01-29

## Hang Seng Debuts Gold ETF with Ethereum-Based Tokenized Units
In a landmark move for Asian markets, Hang Seng has launched a Gold ETF featuring Ethereum-based tokenized units. This marks a significant milestone in the integration of traditional commodities and public blockchain infrastructure, allowing for instantaneous settlement and enhanced liquidity.
[Source: The Block](https://www.theblock.co/)

## US Senators Press Deputy AG Over Alleged Crypto Conflicts
A group of US Senators has formally questioned Deputy Attorney General Blanche regarding potential conflicts of interest related to past legal work in the crypto sector. The inquiry highlights the increasing political scrutiny and the high stakes of regulatory appointments as the US moves toward a comprehensive digital asset framework.
[Source: The Block](https://www.theblock.co/)

## UAE Central Bank Sanctions First US Dollar Stablecoin
The UAE Central Bank has officially sanctioned its first US Dollar-backed stablecoin, designed to streamline digital asset settlements across the region. This move positions the UAE as a leading global hub for regulated stablecoin activity and institutional digital finance.
[Source: The Block](https://www.theblock.co/)

## Grayscale Predicts End of the "Four-Year Cycle" in 2026
In its latest 2026 Digital Asset Outlook, Grayscale argues that the traditional four-year crypto cycle is coming to an end. Driven by persistent institutional inflows and macro demand for alternative stores of value, Grayscale predicts Bitcoin could reach new all-time highs in the first half of 2026.
[Source: Grayscale Research](https://research.grayscale.com/reports/2026-digital-asset-outlook-dawn-of-the-institutional-era)

## Russia to Roll Out Comprehensive Crypto Framework by July
Russia is set to introduce a full regulatory framework for cryptocurrencies by July 2026, which will notably allow for retail participation. The move signals a shift in the global regulatory landscape as more nations seek to capitalize on digital asset growth while maintaining oversight.
[Source: The Block](https://www.theblock.co/)
