# Crypto Daily Insight - 2026-01-27

## Silver & Gold Outperform Bitcoin Amid Macro Uncertainty
Bitcoin (BTC) continues to struggle around the $88,000 mark, while silver has surged past $115, overtaking Bitcoin's post-2017 percentage gains. This trend underscores a growing preference for traditional commodities as safe havens while markets await the next Federal Reserve rate decision and monitor government shutdown risks.
[Source: The Block](https://www.theblock.co/post/silver-overtakes-bitcoins-post-2017-gains)

## First Avalanche ETF Hits US Markets
VanEck has officially launched the first Avalanche (AVAX) ETF in the United States. This marks a significant milestone for the Layer-1 ecosystem, following the success of Bitcoin and Ethereum ETFs. The move is expected to bring institutional-grade liquidity to the Avalanche network.
[Source: The Block](https://www.theblock.co/post/first-avalanche-etf-hits-us-markets)

## Polymarket Secures Exclusive MLS Licensing Deal
Polymarket, the leading decentralized prediction market, has signed an exclusive licensing deal with Major League Soccer (MLS). This partnership will allow Polymarket to offer unique data-driven betting markets for US soccer, further bridging the gap between mainstream sports and decentralized finance (DeFi).
[Source: The Block](https://www.theblock.co/post/polymarket-signs-exclusive-licensing-deal-with-mls)

## Bitwise Launches On-Chain Vault via Morpho
Bitwise has debuted its first on-chain vault using the Morpho protocol, targeting yields of up to 6% on USDC. This institutional-grade DeFi product demonstrates the ongoing shift of traditional asset managers toward permissionless, transparent financial infrastructure.
[Source: The Block](https://www.theblock.co/post/bitwise-debuts-onchain-vault-via-morpho)

## BitMine Executes Massive Ethereum Purchase
Ethereum treasury firm BitMine has made its largest purchase of 2026, acquiring over 40,000 ETH. The firm now holds and stakes over 2 million tokens, signaling strong institutional confidence in Ethereum's long-term value despite recent price volatility below $3,000.
[Source: CoinDesk](https://www.coindesk.com/)
